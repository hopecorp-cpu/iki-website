How much time do I need each day?

Just 30 seconds to log your journal. The AI handles the analysis. You do not need to change your whole lifestyle at once — IKI walks with you one small step at a time.

I am taking Western medication — can I still use it?

Yes. IKI focuses on nutrition, lifestyle and wellness — it is a complementary approach, not a replacement for medication. Any change to your medication should be discussed with your doctor.
