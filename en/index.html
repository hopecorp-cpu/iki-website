I'm short on time — how many minutes per day do I need?

Just 30 seconds/day for journaling. The AI handles the analysis. When enrolled in a course, plan an extra 30 minutes/day for 7 days to watch the lessons. After that, it's about applying it to your life.

Am I currently taking medication — should I stop?

No. IKI is a complement, not a replacement for medical care. Any medication change should be discussed with your treating physician. IKI focuses on nutrition, movement, breath, and lifestyle — the foundational pillars that every doctor encourages.
