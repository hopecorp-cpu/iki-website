Yin-Yang · Five Elements

Foundational principles of Eastern medicine · 2000+ years

The theories of Yin-Yang (I Ching, ~3000 BC) and Five Elements (Huang Di Nei Jing, ~200 BC) — public-domain heritage of Eastern civilization. The core principles that IKI uses to build the 5-constitution framework: Cold / Hot / Deficient / Excess / Damp.

14th century · ~1330–1400
📜

Tue Tinh

Father of Vietnamese traditional medicine

Author of "Nam Duoc Than Hieu" — the work that established Vietnam's traditional medical system using Vietnamese herbs. The philosophy "Vietnamese medicine for Vietnamese people" runs through IKI's spirit.

18th century · 1720–1791
📚

Hai Thuong Lan Ong

Le Huu Trac · Vietnam's great physician

Author of "Hai Thuong Y Tong Tam Linh" — a 28-volume Vietnamese traditional medicine encyclopedia, crystallizing knowledge of constitutions, pathology, pharmacology, and longevity practices. The central knowledge source for IKI's personalization framework.

Current scientific advisor

Dr. Ngo Duc Vuong

Scientific advisor to HOPE CORP — systematizes Yin-Yang/Five Elements and Vietnamese traditional medicine (Tue Tinh, Hai Thuong Lan Ong) into IKI's training programs and knowledge base.

HOPE CORP is the legal entity owning both the IKI brand and the "Ngo Duc Vuong" trademark — operating with transparency and accountability to customers, partners, and community.

Compliance note: IKI Academy's knowledge base is for lifestyle & wellness education — not medical advice and not a substitute for diagnosis/treatment by licensed healthcare professionals.
